Our Story

Crafting Dreams Since 2016

Meet Our Founder

Bold Couture was founded by Miss Tebello Zacharia in 2016, with a vision to redefine luxury through handcrafted accessories and loungewear. What began as a passion for creating unique, high-quality pieces has evolved into a brand synonymous with elegance and comfort.

With a background in fashion design and a keen eye for detail, Miss Zacharia has built Bold Couture into a respected name in the fashion industry, creating pieces that combine traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.

Tebello Zacharia

Founder & Creative Director

Our Mission

At Bold Couture, our mission is to provide high-quality, handmade products that inspire confidence and creativity. We believe in the power of craftsmanship and strive to make each piece a statement of individuality and style.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual can express their unique style through our thoughtfully designed products, creating a community of bold, fashion-forward thinkers.
